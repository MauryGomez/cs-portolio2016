Vindinium

Vindinium is a turn based game, in which the goal is to have the most amount of gold in the set amount of rounds. Everyround you gain gold accord to the number of mines you own, if an enemy bot were to you they gain control of all the mines you owned. When you attack a mine to take control of it you lose health, you also lose health everytime the bot moves. To gain health back the bot will need to go to a tavern. The bots are only controlled by the code that makes them up, whether it be to only attack enemies, mine or both. The Vindinium map is randoomly cycled every round, from big to small maps with different amounts of mine and taverns depending on the size of the map.
